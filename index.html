HACS is an invitation-only workshop aiming at improving the quality of cryptographic software. The workshop has been run three times so far, in January 2016, 2017 and 2018. It is typically co-located with the IACR Real-World Crypto Symposium. Founders of the workshop are Gilles Barthe, Ben Laurie, and Trevor Perrin.
